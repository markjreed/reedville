Trivia Championship Questions

These are the questions and answers from the Team Trivia championship match for the first season of 2007, held April 15th, 2007 at Medieval Times Discover Mills. Our team came in 5th out of 150, and would have tied for third if we had listened to the all-knowing Seannibal a few more times.

Rules

The questions are grouped into blocks of three. Within each block there are three point values (low, medium, and high), which must each be assigned to exactly one question in the block. But the team must submit each answer with its point value in sequence. So they only know the categories for the remaining question(s) in the group at the time that they choose the point value for each of the first two questions.

Each round ended with a single "long-form" question, where the answer is a list and each item of the list is worth a certain number of points.

Round One

The point values for this round were 1, 3, and 5.

No Category Question
1 Sitcoms What show is the only live-action sitcom which debuted in the 90's and is still on the air?
2 Landmarks At the entrance to which famous U.S. landmark will you find the poem The New Colossus?
3 Food According to the directions on the box, for how long - within three seconds - should you heat a Kelloggs® Pop-TartTM in a microwave oven set on high?
4 Music Besides all being dead, what specifically do all four people mentioned in the REM song The End of the World (As We Know It) have in common?
5 Vocabulary What eight-letter word is the shortest one in English to include all six letters A, B, C, D, E, and F?
Hint: of the additional two letters in the word, one is a repetition of one of those letters, while the other is from elsewhere in the alphabet.
6 Movie Sequels What film holds the record for the longest period of time between its initial theatrical release and that of its first sequel?
7 Sports It was the top of the fourth inning, one on, nobody out. The ball was pitched by Al Downing, hit over the head of Bill Buckner, and landed in the bullpen, where it was retrieved by reliever Tom House. What was the occasion?
8 Miscellaneous Things Where would you find the Malphigian Pyramids?
  1. On the surface of Mars
  2. Within the human kidney
  3. At Disney's EPCOT Center World Showcase
  4. In the novel Dune
9 Military One branch of the US Armed Forces accounts for more than 50% of the total US military consumption of fossil fuels. Which branch?

Long-Form Question

20 List all of the full-length animated features ever theatrically released by Disney and/or Pixar whose title consists of only a single word. Scoring: +2 points for each correct entry; -3 points for each incorrect entry.

Note: Articles count as words, so any title of the form The X is out. Tron does not count as "animated".
