Date and Time on Cindu

Cindu is a fictional planet created by Roger Mills as the home of the speakers of his constructed languages Kash, Gwr, and Prevli. This page converts dates between our calendar and theirs.

Instructions

Change any field in either date and instantly see the corresponding date in the other calendar. Use the <- and -> buttons to go backward and forward by days; click Noon to set the time to noon on the given date.

Changing the date stops the clock; click Now to jump back to the present and start it again.

Notes

Current time is based on your computer's clock and is only as accurate as that clock is. Similarly, the software's idea of Local Time vs UTC (a.k.a. GMT) is based on whatever time zone your PC thinks it's in.

Certain aspects of modern rules are applied throughout time, even though this disagrees with historical fact in some cases. For instance, the Earth year is always considered to start on January 1st. Daylight Saving Time determination uses the rules currently in place in your locale; for reliable historical results down to the time of day, convert to UTC, which ignores Daylight Saving Time.

Cindu Time is in their analog to GMT, local time at zero degrees longitude.
